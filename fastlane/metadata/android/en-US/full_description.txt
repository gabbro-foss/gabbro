Gabbro is a free, open-source password manager for people who take security seriously. Your secrets are protected by memory-hard key derivation (Argon2id) and AES-256-GCM encryption - both resistant to classical and quantum attack.

Named after the intrusive igneous rock: hard, stable, enduring.

Features:

* Quantum-resistant by design - vault security rests on Argon2id + AES-256-GCM.
* Optional hardware key - FIDO2/YubiKey authentication (passphrase-only by default; two keys minimum when keys are used).
* Rust for all secrets - every cryptographic operation lives in Rust; secrets never cross the Flutter/Rust bridge in plaintext.
* Local-first - your vault lives on your device; sync is your choice and your responsibility.
* No telemetry - no analytics, no accounts, no tracking; works fully offline.
* Localised - UI available in many languages, following your system locale with an in-app override.
* Multi-language passphrase generator - wordlists across many languages, plus a script-aware classic generator.
* Offline in-app help - no external website or internet connection required.
* FOSS - GPL-3.0-only.

Alpha release: the cryptographic implementation has not yet undergone external review. It is provided as-is, without warranty, as stated in the GPL-3.0.
